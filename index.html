About Me

David Peletz is an ACE Certified Personal Trainer with a qualification in TRX Suspsension Training. David graduated from Lick-Wilmerding Class of 2016 and is a member of Macalester College's Class of 2020. He plans on pursuing a major in Computer Science with a minor in Data Science. He also plans on taking classes in Kinesiology at the University of Minnesota. David has been trained in Muay Thai.
